About Me

I am a junior at Brown University. My recent projects include a user experience study for a physical interface, a responsive website, a larger scale group project to practice iterating on design ideas, and an interactive sorting and filtering website built in React.js. These projects follow my personal development as a designer. In chronological order, they build from the fundamentals of a physical user interface and up to incorporating more and more engineering to bring the websites to life.

The process of design is very rewarding for me. I think there needs to be consideration of a variety of potential users at every step in the design process. From initial sketches to wireframes, lofi and highfi prototypes, and finally to the actual coded website, it is important to think about what different users would want from the product and be agile in its development.
